LIST ALL YOUR FAVORITE SERIES / ANIME / MOVIES / BOOKS / GAMES AND NEVER GET LOST

MyLists has a beautiful and clear interface which allows you to list all your series / anime / movies / books/ games without remembering what was the last watched episode. It integrates all kind of statistics so you can see how much time you spent. Moreover, you can see your follows' lists and compare it to yours.

COMPARE YOUR LIST WITH OTHER USERS

MyLists includes a Hall of Fame of all registered users. By default, everyone can see everyone's list, last updates, time spent, favorites and much more.
